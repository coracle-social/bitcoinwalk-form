Getting Started Guide

1.

BitcoinWalk uses Flotilla, which is powered by nostr.

2.

First, pick a signer app (to keep your nostr key safe):

  • On desktop, use Peridot
  • On Android, use Amber
  • On iOS, use Aegis
  • Or, choose another option.
3.

Install Flotilla or visit it in your web browser:

Web • Android • iOS
4.

Sign in with your signer.

5.

Join a group!
